# Privacy Policy for Mind Vault

**Effective Date:** December 7, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Mind Vault ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that our application **Mind Vault** is designed to prioritize your privacy and security by operating entirely offline on your device.

## 2. Data Collection
We **do not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, passwords, master seeds, or usage data. 

- **No Remote Servers:** The application does not connect to any external server to process your data.
- **Local Processing:** All password generation happens locally on your device using the SHA-256 algorithm.
- **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

## 3. Data Storage
Mind Vault is a stateless application. It does not store your "Master Seed" or generated passwords on your device's persistent storage. Once you close the application or the memory is cleared, your data is gone.

## 4. Permissions
The application requests the following permission only for the specific purpose listed:
- **Internet:** Technically enabled by the underlying web view framework, but **not used** by the application logic to send data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us via our GitHub repository.
